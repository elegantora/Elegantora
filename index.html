The Anatomy of a
Masterpiece

Fragrance is a symphony that unfolds over time. To truly appreciate Elegantora, one must understand the three layers of the olfactive pyramid.

01. The Opening (Top Notes)

The immediate impression. Usually volatile molecules like Citrus and Light Berries. They last 5-15 minutes, inviting you into the story.

02. The Heart (Middle Notes)

The soul of the perfume. These emerge after the top notes dissipate and define the personality. Florals and spices usually reside here.

03. The Dry Down (Base Notes)

The foundation. Heavy molecules like Musk, Oud, and Amber. These provide the 24-hour longevity Elegantora is known for.

The Royal Concierge

Common questions answered by our master perfumers.

How do I ensure my scent lasts all day?

To maximize longevity, apply your Elegantora perfume to pulse points: wrists, neck, and behind the ears. For an even more lasting sillage, we recommend moisturizing your skin before application, as hydrated skin holds fragrance molecules significantly longer.

What is the difference between Parfum and Cologne?

The difference lies in the concentration of essential oils. While typical Colognes contain 2-5% oil, Elegantora specializes in 'Extrait de Parfum', featuring 20-30% concentration. This is why our scents possess such profound depth and staying power.

Are these fragrances safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. We use natural botanical extracts and high-grade alcohol bases. However, we always recommend a patch test on a small area of skin before full application to ensure complete compatibility with your unique skin chemistry.

Do you offer international shipping for the royal collection?

Indeed. Elegantora ships to over 50 countries worldwide. Our logistics partners ensure that your precious cargo is handled with the utmost care, arriving in a temperature-controlled environment to preserve the integrity of the oils.

THE JOURNEY OF A DROP

A Global Olfactory Map

We traverse the most remote corners of the Earth to bring you ingredients that cannot be found in ordinary perfumery.

Bulgaria • Rose Valley

Our Damask Roses are harvested at dawn, between 5:00 AM and 7:00 AM, to ensure the dew remains on the petals, preserving the highest oil concentration possible.

Cambodia • Pursat Forest

The source of our wild Agarwood (Oud). We only use trees that have aged for over 50 years, giving our Oud a smokiness that is deep, resinous, and eternal.

Italy • Calabria

The world's finest Bergamot. Our cold-pressed extraction method ensures that the zesty, sparkling top notes of our citrus scents remain crisp for hours.

Madagascar • Sambava

Our Vanilla is hand-pollinated and sun-cured for months. This creates the creamy, balsamic undertone found in our Royal Vanilla and DareGirl editions.

Ethical Opulence

Luxury should never come at the cost of the Earth. Elegantora is committed to 100% cruelty-free testing and sustainable harvesting. We partner with local farmers to ensure that for every 100 bottles produced, we fund the replanting of rare botanical species in their native habitats.
