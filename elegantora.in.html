Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +
Simply click the "Order via WhatsApp" button on your favorite perfume. It will open your WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Send that to us, and we will guide you through the payment and shipping details.
How long does the fragrance last? +
Our perfumes are 'Extrait de Parfum' grade, typically lasting between 20 to 26 hours depending on skin type and environmental conditions.
What are your shipping charges? +
We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹599. For smaller orders, a flat fee of ₹60 applies across India.
Are these original fragrances? +
Yes, Elegantora creates original, proprietary blends inspired by international luxury standards but crafted locally with premium ingredients.
